About 💁

Hello! I'm Joseph R. Lewis. I am a Staff Technical Writing Manager at Google, leading the documentation strategy for AI, Security, and Identity on the Android platform. With over a decade of leadership at Google, I manage a team of expert technical writers and drive cross-functional initiatives to architect complex developer narratives. My career spans 25 years as a software developer, architect, and researcher across the security, privacy, and research domains. I am the author of several technical books, including AdvancED CSS.

Explore my writing portfolio or connect with me on LinkedIn.

In addition to my technical career, I am a professionally trained classical double bassist with over 30 years of performance experience. I have performed with the Boston Symphony, San Francisco Symphony, and alongside artists such as Dave Brubeck and Joshua Bell. I am also the co-founder of the award-winning Lewis Music Studio. My hobbies include mountain biking and studying Mandarin language.
